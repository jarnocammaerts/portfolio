Who Am I

My name is Jarno Cammaerts. I am a multinational person who loves online media. I found my expertise in Web Design and Photography.

Let me tell you a little bit about me. I am Belgian and grew up in Switzerland, where I developed my passion for nature photography. I attended the International School of Basel and later went on to study at Maastricht University and Zuyd Hogeschool.Â  Skills? Well, I am quadrilingual (Dutch, English, French and German). I consider myself a good photographer and webdesigner. But most importantly, I love learning new things.
I currently live in Maastricht and attend Zuyd Hogeschool where I study 'Communication and Multimedia Design'.

Method of work

Define

The first stage of Web Design is defining the project. I make it a major priority to know exactly what a client intends with their project in order to keep the style consistent and give the client exactly what they need.

Design

The second stage is Design. Before the website is built using code, it is imperative that the complete website is designed and approved by the client. This simplifies the work and the overall communication.
